FAQ

  • What is ETHPorto?

    ETHPorto is an independently organized event with a Conference, workshops and a 48-hour Hackathon running in parallel.

  • What does the program look like?

    The Conference and workshops will take place on 16 and 17 March, where the morning of the 16th will be dedicated to Portuguese-only content. In the afternoon of the 16th and from then onwards, everything will be in English. The Hackathon will run in parallel and starts on the morning of March 16th and lasts 48 hours until the morning of March 18th.

  • When should I arrive?

    You are welcome to come any time, but the event starts on March 16th, so plan to arrive at least on the 15th. It will finish on the evening of March 18th. Portugal has a lot to offer, so if time allows, stay a little longer.

  • Can I hack and attend the rest of the event too?

    Yes, you can hack and listen to the Conference or attend the hands-on workshops whenever you’d like.
